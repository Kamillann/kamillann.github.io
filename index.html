Welcome to an unhinged review of shitty interior design

Buckle up, because this isn’t your average Pinterest-perfect interior design blog. No, my dear tasteless adventurer, this is a shrine to the loud, the mismatched, and the downright absurd. Think leopard print couches clashing with paisley wallpaper, glitter lava lamps on marble pedestals, and a chandelier made of plastic forks that screams "I peaked in 1978." Here, we don’t just embrace bad taste—we roll it in gold spray paint, slap a fake diamond on it, and hang it in the middle of the room for everyone to admire. If you’ve ever wondered how far a crime against decor can go, you’re about to find out. Welcome home.

Tiny Rugs Make Me Violently Angry

Nothing makes a room look more disjointed than a postage stamp-sized rug floating in the middle of your floor. Rugs are supposed to anchor your space, not leave it flailing like a toddler in an oversized shirt. Measure your space. Buy a real rug. It’s not hard.

Shiplap is a Disease, and We Need a Cure

Why does every home have to look like Joanna Gaines sneezed on it? Shiplap has officially overstayed its welcome. You’re not in a rustic farmhouse; you live in a suburban split-level! Stop plastering your walls with planks like you're preparing for a barn-raising. Let’s give drywall its dignity back.
